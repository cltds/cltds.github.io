Bio

Lauritz Gerlach is an alumnus of Bucerius Law School (Class of 2018) and has worked for his alma mater's Legal Technology department since 2017. From 2019 to 2020, he was Assistant Director Legal Technology. Since the inception of the center in 2020, he has been Assistant Director of the Bucerius Center for Legal Technology and Data Science.
